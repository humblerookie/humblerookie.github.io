ABOUT ME

Hi, I create and design stuff around Android.


I currently work at Craftsvilla as a Sr. Android developer. In the past I've worked with a few startups mainly around mobile and web, including a unusually long sting at a corporate MNC. I have most of my stuff on Github. I'm a Mumbai University 2011 Information Technology alumni and I'm based out of Mumbai for a while.


I dish out code during the day and electronic bullets by the night. I love wildlife documentaries and a big admirer of wild cats. House MD is my favorite.


"George Carlin made me smile, Jimmy Carr makes me laugh."

RESUME

Experience

  • Web Developer Nov 2011 - Mar 2012
    NH7

    I built stuff in PHP and did a bunch of projects revolving around ecommerce. Built some admin panels, wrote scripts to facilitate some backend jobs and developed their shopping cart using prestashop for their music store.

  • Web / Android Developer April 2012 - Feb 2013
    TCS

    I developed web services and a few web applications for consumption that were focused on document management and other backend applications for our client Citibank. Additionally was involved in interacting with IBM's messed up ecosystem of products from Websphere, RAD and others.

      Mar 2013 - Aug 2014

    I managed to get into their mobility division and built android apps for marathons like New York, Amsterdam, Berlin, Mumbai, Bangalore. These had typical features like live tracking, race predictor and some other API consuming feature.I've also worked on an app built for a former IPL franchise Rajasthan Royals.

  • Software Developer Sept 2014 - Jun 2015
    Hipcask

    I worked as an android dev and built their app which was a typical API consuming client. Additionally was involved in building their backend stack whis was on PHP. I also built their pallete profiling algorithms that give better recommendations to users

  • Android Developer July 2015 - Oct 2016
    Super Jobs

    I worked as an android dev and built their core appmodules. Additionally built their peer to peer chat around quickblox. Worked on app performance, optimizing and memory management.

  • Sr. Android Developer Nov 2016 - present
    Craftsvilla

    I work here building and consuming apis and trying to make build a more performant app.

Education

  • Bachelor of Engineering 2008 - 2011
    Mumbai University

    I studied Information Technology at VCET Vasai. My final year project was a distributed Intrusion detection System.
